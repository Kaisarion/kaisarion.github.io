Fourth Amendment Investgiation

In an age of continued technological advancements, the previously-defined US Constitution begins to lack coverage for the digitally-stored intellectual property and behavior of individuals. From its potential to both bring people closer and aid in solving some of the world's most unprecedented crises, while also serving as an aid for terrorism and severe cause to a human right of privacy to decline, it has contributed towards growth of unwarranted government intrusion. But how are these now-online aspects of human life covered by the enacted Fourth Amendment, granting protection to American citizens?
